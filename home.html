Our Mission

This is our mission. There is only one world and we must share it equally. However, due to the our expansion,weather it be industrial or personal use, the Earth is slowly reaching its capcity. Wildlife is disappearing. Ocean levels are rising. The population of animals are decreasing termendously. Mother Nature needs us more then ever.

How You Can Help

The fate of the world depends on the people's hands. Bringing awareness to our surrounding issues, ranging from global warming to the decrease in wildlife, the Earth needs your help. EcoHarmony provides our users to post their findings and their belifs about nature as well as ways you can start caring for our beloved Planet Earth.
